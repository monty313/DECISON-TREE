05Verdict

The Definition of Done — 90% on MIN(IS,OOS) — is not achievable on this data. Proof stack, each leg independent:

① Requirement vs achievement: 90% of traded days needs p ≥ 0.63–0.82 at RR 2.5 (structure-dependent); eleven selection methods across two sessions measure p = 0.20–0.30 at any usable coverage. ② Coverage cap: the compliant license fires on ~50% of days, so 90% of all days is impossible even if every licensed trade won. ③ Day geometry: 4% trailing DD with 1% risk allows ~3 losses per day; no measured win rate survives that budget. ④ Frontier flatness: tightening gates buys ~1pt of win rate per 20pts of coverage — far too little slope to ever reach the requirement.

What would change the answer: an input with real predictive content at this horizon — order-flow/tick microstructure, ES/NQ/VIX cross-asset leads, news-calendar timing — none of which exists in M1 OHLCV.

06What the mission did deliver

  • s11.py — an honest FTMO day-scorer: next-bar fills, 3-point spread, day-independent scoring, path-accurate trailing DD (synthetic halt level, DD-before-target inside each bar), ≤1% risk, consistency metrics. Any future idea can be scored truthfully in seconds.
  • s11_diag.py — the requirement-curve tool: tells you before building a strategy what win rate its trigger density must deliver.
  • A refusal machine that works — the gravity gates are genuinely selective and the DD-safety layer held zero breaches in compliant configs. The machine is safe; the market at this resolution offers it nothing to harvest after costs.
  • Five documented bug classes that manufacture fake edges (R-normalization, no-stop exits, stale fills, frictionless spread, compounding inflation) — the checklist that separates every prior "breakthrough" from reality.

07Method notes

  • Data: 6 years US30 M1 OHLCV (2020-07 → 2026-05), ~2.0M bars, ~400k M5 decision bars. Closed bars only; HTF features from the last closed HTF bar; no look-ahead anywhere.
  • Costs: 3.0 index-point spread on every fill (mission floor); slippage 0 beyond next-bar-open fills, stated assumption.
  • Protocol: all tuning on DEV (2020-07…2024-06); final OOS (2024-07…2026-05, 264+ trading days ≥ 30 required) evaluated once, at the end. Full 6-year runs as robustness check (A: 13.6% traded / avg −0.10R; B: 16.2% / −0.14R).
  • Day definition: every calendar day with market data counts; a day passes only by locking +2.5% of initial balance without the trailing 4% daily DD ever firing (checked on balance and floating equity, DD before target). Both traded-day and all-day rates reported everywhere.
